# v1.5.9 — Double-Torus Bottle Adapter

**Date:** Feb 22 – Mar 30, 2026

## Summary

Original liquid bait station design using a threaded bottle adapter with double-torus
reservoir. A standard bottle screws into the station upside-down; bait wicks through
the torus channels to a tray where ants feed.

## Key design elements

- Threaded bottle adapter with lead-in cut for insertion
- Double-torus reservoir with backstops and flow control
- O-ring groove for liquid seal
- Tube arm with lip for o-ring seating
- Three-arm support structure

## Outcome

Abandoned in favor of the v2 rigid-reservoir design. Flexible retail bottles caused
uncontrolled fluid rush — the bottle walls collapsed under vacuum, flooding the tray.
The v2 clam-shell design replaces the bottle with a rigid 3D-printed reservoir and
push-pin activated TPU valve.
